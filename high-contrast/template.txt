For a full width content bar, use this template

<div class="channel-bar">
    <span class="channel-number">
        <p>XX NAME</p>
    </span>
    <span class="channel-content">
        CONTENT    
    </span>
</div>

For two half width content bars, use this template

<div class="channel-bar">
    <span class="channel-number">
        <p>XX NAME</p>
    </span>
    <span class="channel-content-half">
        CONTENT    
    </span>
    <span class="channel-content-half">
        CONTENT    
    </span>
</div>
